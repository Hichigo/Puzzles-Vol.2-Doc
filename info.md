# General information

## Plugins

For more convenient configuration of **BP\_Puzzle\_4** and **BP\_Puzzle\_5**, the **Editor Utility Widget** was made to work, you need to enable the **Editor Scripting Utilities** plugin.

In the menu bar, click **Edit -> Plugins** and in the search bar enter the name of the plugin.

![](<.gitbook/assets/image (2).png>)

After enabling the plugin, restart the project.
